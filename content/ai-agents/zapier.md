# Difference between Zapier AI Agents and AI by Zapier 🔀

## ✦ Zapier AI Agents: Autonomous Problem Solvers

Zapier AI Agents act as independent digital teammates within your support inbox. Instead of following rigid, pre-programmed paths, they use natural language processing and advanced reasoning to understand a customer's intent, handle ambiguity, and determine the best sequence of actions to take. 

By connecting an AI Agent to your knowledge base and your tech stack, it can converse naturally with customers, independently look up live information (like shipping updates or subscription statuses), and trigger resolutions on its own. It is the perfect solution for dynamic, unpredictable customer conversations that require flexible decision-making.

## ✦ AI by Zapier: Smart, Rule-Based Automation

While AI Agents handle the conversation, **AI by Zapier** operates behind the scenes as a powerful utility step within your traditional Zapier workflows (Zaps). It allows you to inject artificial intelligence directly into deterministic, background processes without needing a separate API key for OpenAI or Anthropic.

For example, when a new support email arrives, AI by Zapier can automatically scan the text, determine the sentiment (angry, confused, satisfied), extract the customer's order number, and route that structured data to the correct department's Google Sheet or CRM. It is a targeted, single-purpose tool designed to process data efficiently.

## ⚖️ Zapier AI Agents vs. AI by Zapier: Which Should You Use?

Understanding the difference between the two comes down to **Autonomy vs. Utility**. 

* **Choose Zapier AI Agents** when you need a conversational, customer-facing bot that can adapt to context, ask clarifying questions, and dynamically choose which apps to interact with to solve a customer's problem. 
* **Choose AI by Zapier** when you need a highly predictable, hidden background process that simply requires a touch of AI to parse, summarize, or format data moving from one app to another.

In a fully optimized omnichannel inbox, you can use both: AI Agents to handle the live chat, and AI by Zapier to quietly organize the data generated from those conversations.
