Getting started with Handoff is quick and easy. Follow these steps to create your account and organization:

1. Navigate to the **Sign Up** page on the Handoff web app. If you're on the **Sign In** page, click **Register** to go the sign-up page.
1. Click **Sign In with Google**. OR
1. Sign up with email address and password:
  1. Enter your **Email Address**.
  1. Enter **Password**.
  1. Enter same password in **Confirm password**.
  1. Click **Register**.
  1. Check your email inbox for a verification link. You must verify your email address to secure your account.
1. Once logged in, we will create an **Organization** for you. This represents your business and is where all your channels, agents, and team members will be grouped.

*Note: If you have been invited to an existing organization by a team member, you can accept the invitation from the Invitations page after signing up.*
