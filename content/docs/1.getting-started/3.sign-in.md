To access your inbox and settings:

1. Go to the **Sign In** page.
1. If you signed up using your Google Account, click **Sign In with Google**.
2. If you signed up with email address and password:
  1. Enter the email address and password you used during registration.
  1. Click **Sign In**.
  1. If you have forgotten your password, use the **Forgotten Password?** link on the sign-in page to receive a reset link via email.
4. You will be redirected to your primary **Inbox** view, where you can see your active threads and unread messages.
