To securely log out of your account, especially when using a shared or public computer:

1. Open the user menu by clicking on your **Avatar/Profile Picture** in the bottom left or top right corner of the navigation drawer.
2. Select **Log Out** from the dropdown menu.
3. You will be securely logged out and redirected to the login screen.
