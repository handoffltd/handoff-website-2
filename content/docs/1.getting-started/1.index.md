Handoff is an AI-first omni-channel inbox designed to centralize your customer communications. Whether your customers reach out via WhatsApp, Instagram, Messenger, Telegram, or SMS, Handoff brings all those conversations into a single, unified interface.

By connecting powerful AI agents (like OpenAI or Microsoft Copilot Studio) to your communication channels, you can automate front-line support, answer common questions instantly, and seamlessly escalate complex issues to human agents.

## Key Capabilities

* **Omni-Channel Inbox**: Manage conversations from multiple platforms in one place.
* **AI-First Support**: Route incoming messages to AI agents to handle Level-1 support automatically.
* **Seamless Escalation**: Configure rules and prompts to hand off conversations from the AI to a human seamlessly.
* **Rich Messaging**: Support for attachments, images, videos, audio, and rich Markdown text.
* **Team Collaboration**: Invite members to your organization and share the inbox.
