Your personal settings control how your account looks and behaves. 

## Avatar & Name

- Navigate to the user menu and select **Settings > Personal**.
- You can modify your avatar or display name on this page.


## Theme Preference

- Navigate to the user menu and open **Appearance** sub-menu.
- You can set your preference for light, dark or system theme in this menu option.
