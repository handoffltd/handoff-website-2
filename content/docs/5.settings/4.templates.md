Handoff supports two distinct types of templates: **Session Templates** and **WhatsApp Templates**. Templates allow your team to initiate WhatsApp messages with pre-approved WhatsApp templates or respond quickly with session templates. 

---

## Session Templates

Session Templates are standard canned responses you can use when replying from the inbox (within an active session window for providers requiring a session window) to avoid typing repeated responses.

### Creating and Editing Session Templates

* Navigate to the user menu and select **Templates > Session Templates**.
* **Create New**: Click **New Template** and provide a body text and a name. Template names are automatically formatted to lowercase alphanumeric characters with underscores to ensure consistency.
* **Search and Filter**: You can search through your templates by name or body text, and filter them by their active/inactive status.
* **Managing Status**: If you no longer need a template but want to keep it for future reference, you can mark it as **Inactive**. Inactive templates are filtered out of the list of templates displayed to agents in the inbox.
* **Delete**: You can permanently delete a session template if it is no longer needed. **This action cannot be undone**.

---

## WhatsApp Templates

WhatsApp Templates are managed on [WhatsApp Manager](https://business.facebook.com/wa/manage/){target="_blank"} but must be synced to Handoff before you can use them. Approved templates can be used to initiate conversations or reply outside the standard 24-hour session window.

### Syncing from WhatsApp Manager

Because WhatsApp templates require approval from Meta, you must pull them directly from your connected [WhatsApp Manager](https://business.facebook.com/wa/manage/){target="_blank"}:

1. Click **Sync from WhatsApp Manager**.
1. Select the specific WhatsApp Channel (number) you want to fetch templates for and click **Fetch Templates**.
1. The system will retrieve your templates, displaying their category, language, and approval status.
1. Click **Import** on the templates you wish to use within Handoff.

### Managing Imported WhatsApp Templates

* **Status Syncing**: Templates that are "APPROVED" in WhatsApp Manager are imported as **Active** by default. Unapproved templates default to Inactive.
* **Local Controls**: You can manually toggle an imported WhatsApp template to **Inactive** or **Active** within Handoff, or delete it from your local workspace.
* **Search and Preview**: Like session templates, you can filter imported WhatsApp templates by status or text. A preview card will display exactly how the template components (header, body, footer, buttons) will appear to the user.
