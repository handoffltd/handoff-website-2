Integrate Telegram to communicate with customers who prefer fast, secure messaging. Handoff connects to Telegram via a Telegram Bot.

## Setup Instructions

1. Open the Telegram app and search for the **BotFather** (the official bot creation tool by Telegram).
1. Send the command `/newbot` and follow the prompts to choose a name and username for your new bot.
1. Once created, BotFather will give you an **API Token** (often referred to as a Bot Token). Copy this token.
1. Go back to Handoff and navigate to **Channels** in your user menu.
1. Click **Add Channel** and select **Telegram**.
1. Paste your copied **Bot Token** into the provided field.
1. Click **Connect Bot**. Handoff will automatically configure the required connection with Telegram.
