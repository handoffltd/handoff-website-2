Channels are the gateways through which your customers communicate with your business. Handoff allows you to connect multiple numbers and accounts from various platforms into one workspace.

Navigate to **Channels** in your user menu to manage your integrations.

::alert
---
color: warning
---
#description
Only users with **Owner** or **Admin** role are allowed to add or modify channels.
::

**Supported Channels:**

* [WhatsApp](/docs/channels/whatsapp)
* [Instagram](/docs/channels/instagram)
* [Messenger](/docs/channels/messenger)
* [Telegram](/docs/channels/telegram)
* [Twilio SMS](/docs/channels/twilio)
