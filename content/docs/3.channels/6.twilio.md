Reach customers natively via SMS by connecting your Twilio account to Handoff. This is perfect for traditional text message support and notifications.

## Setup Instructions

1. Log in to your **Twilio Console**.
1. From your Twilio dashboard, locate and copy your **Account SID** and **Auth Token**. You will also need the specific Twilio **Phone Number** you wish to use.
1. In Handoff, go to **Channels** in your user menu.
1. Click **Add Channel** and select **Twilio SMS**.
1. Fill in the required fields:
    * **Account SID**
    * **Auth Token**
1. Click **Fetch Phone Numbers**. 
1. Select a phone number in the result to connect to Handoff.
1. Handoff will automatically configure the required connection with Twilio.
