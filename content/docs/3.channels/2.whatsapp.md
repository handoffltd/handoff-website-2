Integrate your WhatsApp Business account to respond to customers directly from Handoff.

## Setup Instructions

1. Go to **Channels** in your user menu and click **Add Channel**.
1. Select **WhatsApp**.
1. You will be prompted to use the embedded signup flow which requires logging in with your Facebook/Meta App credentials.
1. Go through the wizard in the pop-up to select/create a WhatsApp Business Account and selecting/adding a phone number.
1. Once you complete the pop-up wizard steps, it will close and you'll see the process messages connecting your phone number to Handoff.
1. Click **Done** once the process completes.
