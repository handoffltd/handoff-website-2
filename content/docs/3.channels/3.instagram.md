Handle Instagram Direct Messages directly alongside your other channels.

## Setup Instructions

1. Ensure your Instagram account is set to a **Professional/Business** account (linking to a Facebook Page is not required).
1. Go to **Channels** in your user menu and select **Add Channel > Instagram**.
1. Log in with Facebook to authorize Handoff to access your Instagram messages.
1. Select the specific Instagram account you wish to connect.
1. Save the configuration. Messages sent to your Instagram inbox will now appear in Handoff.
