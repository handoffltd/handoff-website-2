# WhatsApp Integration

Meet your customers exactly where they already spend their time. By integrating WhatsApp with your AI-first inbox, you can deliver instant, personalized support at scale.

## Key Benefits

* **Global Reach:** Connect with billions of active users worldwide using a platform they trust.
* **Rich Media Support:** Send and receive images, documents, audio messages, and interactive buttons to create engaging conversational experiences.
* **AI-Powered Automation:** Let intelligent agents handle routine FAQs, order tracking, and booking inquiries 24/7, smoothly handing off to human agents when complex support is needed.
* **Proactive Engagement:** Send timely notifications, appointment reminders, and promotional broadcasts directly to your customers' lock screens.

Transform your WhatsApp channel into a revenue-generating, customer-delighting powerhouse.
